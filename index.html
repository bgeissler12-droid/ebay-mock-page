Developer note (visible to candidates): This mock mirrors the selector patterns and upload mechanics of eBay's real "Sell your item" form. A few important details:
  • Element IDs follow eBay's s0-1-0-25-5-@SECTION-... hash pattern and are not stable across product categories or sessions. Stable anchors: aria-label, [id*="@SECTION"], and se-field / summary__* classes.
  • Photos are a real <input type="file"> with a drag-and-drop zone — not URL fields. To upload programmatically you'll need to build a DataTransfer, assign to input.files, and dispatch a change event. Setting files without dispatching the event will not register.
  • Description is a contenteditable div, not a textarea — .value = ... will not work.
